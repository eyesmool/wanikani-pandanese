Meaning

Primary

Barbarian

Mnemonic

Red insect s only bite barbarian s. If a red insect bites you then you have barbarian blood! Waaay back a barbarian was your aunt or uncle or great great grand pappy! But the insects kinda hurt, so it's a bit of a downside I guess.

Hints

If you see a red insect, follow it and see who it bites. Then you'll know that person is at least part barbarian.

How sad. You thought they just liked barbarian blood, but turns out they're actually anti-barbarian activists. That's why they've been biting barbarians all along.
